AR Features w/ Food Photography

Investigating the potential mobile AR has to bring food journalism to life!

üôÇ about

Every quarter, the Knight Lab pulls together multidisciplinary teams of students together to collaborate on projects that represent the future of media. I was fortunate enough to be chosen to be part of a 5-member team that was to work on the project "Augmented Reality Features with 3D Food Photography." Throughout the quarter, we experimented with AR and VR and how it can be implemented to showcase various food products. My team and I worked to create an immersive experience built, with the help of Blender, that we later uploaded on Torch, but the application has since then been discontinued. With our extra time, I also looked into creating a more simplified AR experience with the help of Facebook's SparkAR by creating a filter that we hoped would connect the Northwestern student body during COVID.
