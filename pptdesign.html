Marketing Strategy Presentations

The first three presentations were my final projects for IMC 303: Integrated Marketing Communications, taught by Professor Gerry Chiaro; IMC 306: Strategic Communications, taught by Professor Danielle Robinson Bell; IMC 390: Branding for the Digital World, taught by Professor Alejandra Denda. Both were group presentations, but I offered to design sleek and relevant presentation decks and develop the mock ups that were shown throughout the slides to best convey our insights.

Moonrise Social Club

As a long-time follower of @followmario's content on TikTok, I was surprised to come across Mario Moreno's post looking for interns at his newly founded marketing consulting company. The internship seemed up my alley, so I took a chance at applying and sent an email that night! Fortunately, I received a positive response from Mario who later told me that he grew excited to work with me while reading through my email. Over the course of six months, I conducted research on online marketing courses, created the presentation design and built out a portion of Mario's Marketing Foundations E-Learning Masterclass. The course launched on January 2nd at moonrisesocialclub.com and has amassed close to $800 in profit.
