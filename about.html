It all started when I was 6 years old and enamored with High School Musical. I uncovered what I thought was the story of the century about the actors' ages, drew out a magazine spread on printer paper, and stapled everything together to get ready for pub. My makeshift magazines were delivered under my sisters' doors, and that day marked the beginning of my creative career!

While pursuing my degree in Journalism, I gravitated most to the visuals. From video to design, I loved the editing and creative process. As I became more exposed to the design, I grew curious about digital design and pushed myself to learn more about front-end development and UX/UI processes.

Outside of work, I'm interested in children's education, the color yellow, fandom culture, and streaming services.
